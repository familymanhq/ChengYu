## 📖 Idiom Details & Stories

### 1. 对牛弹琴 (duì niú tán qín)
**English Translation/Meaning:** *Playing a lute to a cow — Wasting effort preaching to the wrong audience.*

#### 📜 The Story (故事)
> **Chinese Simplified:**
> 音乐家公明仪对着一只正在吃草的黄牛弹奏高雅的七弦琴。他弹得非常卖力，琴声极美，但那只黄牛只是低着头继续吃草，根本没有任何反应。公明仪明白：牛不是听不到声音，而是它根本无法理解高雅音乐的旋律。
>
> **English Summary:**
> A musician played a beautiful piece of classical music on his lute for a cow. The cow ignored him and continued chewing grass. The musician realized that playing classical music to a cow is a waste of effort, as the audience is completely unsuited for the message.
