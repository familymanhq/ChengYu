The Idiom / 成语解释

Characters
对牛弹琴
Pinyin
duì niú tán qín
Literal Meaning
Playing the lute to a cow
What it means
Sharing something with the wrong audience — someone who can't appreciate it
“ Use it in a sentence / 例句

Talking about your favorite book to someone who never reads is like 对牛弹琴.

(Gēn yī gè bù ài dú shū de rén tán nǐ zuì xǐhuān de shū, jiǎnzhí – shì duì niú tán qín.)

跟一个不爱读书的人谈你最喜欢的书，简直是对牛弹琴。

The Story Spine / 故事大纲

1
Once there was…

A musician who played the most beautiful music in the land.

2
Every day…

He played for anyone who would listen — and everyone loved it.

3
Until one day…

He found a new audience: a cow grazing alone in a meadow.

4
Because of that…

He played his very best songs — and the cow just kept chewing grass.

5
Until finally…

He understood: the cow wasn't being rude — it simply couldn't understand music.

6
And ever since then…

We say 对牛弹琴 — "playing music to a cow" — whenever something is shared with the wrong audience.
